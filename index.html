Project 01

The Legend of Zelda (1986) Remake

We remake the first dungeon of The Legend of Zelda (1986), and customize it with a portal gun! When you reach the original destination, you'll find that there is no triforce, but a portal gun! Grab it and solve the additional puzzles!

Controls

  • Arrow Move
  • X Use Sword
  • Z Use Item
  • Space Swap Items
  • 1 Cheat Mode

How to Play

Navigate through the dungeon, defeat enemies, and find the portal gun to solve creative puzzles across custom-designed rooms.
